How the automation works

Google Apps Script runs a function automatically every day at 8am. It checks your bookings sheet for any confirmed weddings with an anniversary coming up in 7 days or happening today, then sends the email automatically via Gmail and prepares a one-tap WhatsApp link for you to review and send.

Email: Sent fully automatically by Gmail via Apps Script.
WhatsApp: A pre-filled wa.me link is generated — you open it and tap Send. This is a WhatsApp Business limitation; full automation requires the WhatsApp Business API (paid).
Setup steps
1
Open your Google Apps Script
In your Google Sheet, click Extensions → Apps Script. Replace the existing code with the full script below (copy from the appsscript.gs file in your download — the anniversary version).
2
Add your email address and discount
At the top of the script, set your Gmail address and the discount percentage you want to offer couples.
var SENDER_EMAIL = 'quintadaaldeia@gmail.com'; var DISCOUNT = '15%'; var BOOKING_URL = 'https://yoursite.netlify.app/quote.html';
3
Create the daily trigger
In Apps Script, click Triggers (clock icon on the left) → + Add trigger. Set:
Function: checkAnniversaries
Event source: Time-driven
Type: Day timer
Time: 8am – 9am
Then click Save.
4
Add a "Message history" sheet
In your Google Sheet, add a new tab named exactly MessageHistory. The script will automatically log every message sent here, so you always have a record.
5
Test it works
In Apps Script, select the checkAnniversaries function from the dropdown and click Run. Check your email and the MessageHistory sheet for results.
